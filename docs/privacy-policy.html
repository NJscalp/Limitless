Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Limitless (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Limitless app handles your information.

1. Data We Process

Limitless may process the following data on your device:

  • Name and age you enter in the app
  • Face scan images and facial analysis results
  • Selected apps to block and screen time settings
  • Exercise completion, unlock tasks, and streak data
  • Subscription status

2. On-Device Processing

Face scans and exercise verification are processed locally on your device using Apple frameworks such as Vision and AVFoundation. We do not upload your photos or face scan data to our servers.

3. Local Storage

Your app data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s standard storage systems. We do not maintain our own user database for your app content.

4. Camera Usage

The camera is used only for face scans and exercise verification. Camera data is used to detect facial movement and landmarks required for app functionality.

5. Screen Time and App Blocking

Limitless uses Apple’s Screen Time / Family Controls frameworks to let you block apps and manage usage. This information is handled by iOS and remains device-based.

6. Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We may use RevenueCat only to manage and validate subscription status. We do not receive your payment card details.

7. Third Parties

We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party tracking tools. The app may rely on Apple services and RevenueCat for subscription functionality.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

9. Children’s Privacy

Limitless is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Data Deletion

You can remove your data by uninstalling the app or using any reset/delete options available inside the app.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

12. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at: limitlesslockscreen@gmail.com
